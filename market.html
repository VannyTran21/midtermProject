Personal Review

Faneuil Hall Marketplace is one of the historic locations in Boston for it was a meeting place for patriots back in the days of the Stamp Act, and the “tea crisis”. Now it is known as a marketplace which consists of more than 40 local vendors that sell food, gifts and accessories. As for my experience at this marketplace, most of the food vendors sell the 2 signature dishes of Boston, either lobster rolls or clam chowder or both. I of course had to try those out and they for sure did not disappoint. There is a comic shop nearby that you can check out called “Newbury Comics”. They have all sorts of toy figures, plushies, comics, novels, CDs and vinyls; it’s a fairly large shop so if this is something you’re interested in, you would definitely want to spend at least an hour in here.
