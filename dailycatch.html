Personal Review

My second favorite restaurant during this trip is The Daily Catch. Located amongst other several restaurants in Boston’s Little Italy, this small restaurant stands out with its amazing signature dish Aglio Olio served with black pasta. Their black pasta which is made with squid ink is what makes it special since you don’t normally find this type of pasta that often in other restaurants. If you want to try something different than the normal regular pasta, this dish is a must. Despite being quite a small restaurant with very few tables available, the atmosphere is warm and welcoming and if you are lucky and early enough, you won’t have to wait for the next available table.

Where and When

  • Address: 323 Hanover St, Boston, MA 02113
  • Hours: 11am-9pm (to 10pm Friday and Saturday)
