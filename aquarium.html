Personal Review

I must say that this is one of the most impressive aquariums I've ever visited. Opened for more than 50 years, the place has been hosting more than 1.3 million visitors every year. This aquarium has more than 20,000 animals with an impressive cylindrical tank that holds 200,000 gallons of saltwater (that is 40 feet wide and 23 feet tall). They also have a daily feeding dive in this tank from 10am to 12pm if you want to see how their giant sea turtle, Myrtle, is fed and get her shell scrubbed.They also have a touch tank exhibition where you will have a chance to touch living rays and sharks! I found this activity to be very fun and educational as you get to experience how the skin of these animals feels like while being taught about their diets, lifespan and behaviors

Where and When

  • Address: 1 Central Wharf, Boston, MA 02110
  • Hours: Mon-Tue-Wed-Thu-Fri: 9am-5pm, Sat-Sun: 9am-6pm
